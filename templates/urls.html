Semantic Debian

URLs

Note: These are subject to change without much notice at this point.

The webserver on rdf.debian.net uses content negotiation to return a 303 redirect to either a Turtle or RDF/XML representation of the requested resource. In the future, an HTML view may also be provided.

Packaging Project
http://rdf.debian.net/project/<source-pkg-name>
Maintainer (not yet implemented)
http://rdf.debian.net/maintainer/<url-encoded-email>

Examples
